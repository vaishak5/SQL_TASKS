22. From the table, write a SQL query to find those departments where at least two
employees work. Return department id, number of employees.(GROUP BY & HAVING)

QUERY:

SELECT d.dep_id, COUNT(e.emp_name) as empCount FROM department d JOIN employees e ON e.dep_id=d.dep_id
GROUP BY d.dep_id
HAVING COUNT(e.emp_name)>=2;

Output:

dep_id	empCount
--------------------------------
1001	3
2001	5
3001	6
